Responsive Non-Profit Website Redesign

Chaldon Animal Sanctuary ● June 2023 ● 3 Weeks ● Lead UX Designer

The Chaldon Animal Sanctuary case study involved a 3-week sprint project where I led the UX design. We undertook a comprehensive redesign and rebranding initiative to address navigation inconsistencies and improve the website's visual appeal. Through collaboration, user testing, and research, we developed a responsive and user-friendly design that streamlined the donation process. Our focus on enhancing the user experience and supporting the Sanctuary's mission drove the project's success in improving aesthetics, engagement, and facilitating animal rescue and rehoming.

Responsive Government Agency Website Redesig

ed.gov ● May 2023 ● 4 Weeks ● Sole UX/UI Designer

The Government Agency Responsive Web Redesign project transformed the U.S. Department of Education's website in a 4-week sprint. We focused on navigation, content organization, and cross-device functionality. User-centered design principles and usability testing refined the information architecture and created an engaging interface. The project included interface design and responsive layout implementation, resulting in an accessible and visually appealing website that empowers users in accessing educational resources and information.

Mental Wellbeing iOS App

MINDLINK ● Apr 2023 ● 3 Weeks ● Lead UI Designer

MindLink is a mobile application designed to provide individuals struggling with mental health concerns access to tailored resources, support groups, therapists, and self-help materials. The aim of the app is to empower users by offering personalised assistance and fostering a supportive community, ultimately improving their mental well-being. This project was a 3-week sprint where I assumed the role of the Lead UI Designer, however I played a pivotal role in all aspects of the design process, from research and ideation to prototyping and testing.

Mobile Travel App

Tourista ● March 2023 ● 2 Weeks ● Sole UX/UI Designer

The Tourista case study was a dynamic project where I assumed the Sole Designer role, developing all aspects of the project, from User Research to User Interface. The objective was to develop a cutting-edge travel planning app that revolutionises how modern travellers discover, plan, and enjoy their trips.
